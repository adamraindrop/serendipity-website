About Serendipity

Meet Luke.

Serendipity exists because its founder lived the other side of this: buying tools that promised everything and watching them gather dust.

Our founder's journey

Two exits, one lesson.

Luke was one of the owners of Profits4Purpose, a software company that hosted thousands of volunteer events and facilitated tens of millions in global corporate and employee philanthropy. He also owned and operated Poppy Medical, a leading direct-to-consumer medical accessory company. After Profits4Purpose exited to what is now Blackbaud, a $4.1B company, and Poppy Medical was acquired by its market leader, Luke founded Serendipity to help likeminded business leaders grow their own companies.

Two exits taught him the same lesson. Growth does not come from buying tools. It comes from clean data, clear process, and a team that actually uses the system. That is the work most agencies skip, so he built the one that does not.

“If we work together, you will get the version of this work I wished existed when I was in your seat. Come say hi.”
