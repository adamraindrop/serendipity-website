Questions

Straight answers.

What is sales and marketing alignment?

It is getting both teams to operate on shared data, shared definitions, and one pipeline, so leads are worked consistently and leadership can trust the numbers. In practice it means agreed lifecycle stages, a shared qualified-lead definition, clean handoffs, and closed-loop reporting.

What counts as a qualified lead?

Whatever your two teams agree it is, in writing. Our job is to facilitate that definition and build it into HubSpot so it is enforced, not debated.

How do you measure alignment working?

Fewer leads lost in the handoff, faster first touch, and reporting that ties marketing activity to real pipeline and revenue, reviewed together every month.

Do we need our CRM cleaned up first?

Usually yes. Alignment runs on trustworthy data, so it pairs with an implementation or a portal rebuild if the foundation is not solid yet.
